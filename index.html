About



Why we are here for you?

Aegis includes a group of individuals who is willing to provide a ray of hope to those abused women who is facing difficulties in building up a new life... This is the result of realization of the brutal truth that women in India are cruelly abused and denied to live as a human inside our Indian society. We are here for you, to help you, to support you, to guide you and to make live under the sun safely without any fear. We believe in the Power of "Women".



How can we help you?

We are not an organization which take it as a procedure. But we are here to help, considering you as our own family member. We can help you to weave your dreams…!!...Yes...that’s what we mean…!! Giving you all to set a new life…!! We can show you a better world, a world in which you could live as if you are a genuine part of it.



How to get our help?

The only thing you need to do is to fill up the request form given below and register yourself. Your details will be safe with us. We verify the details and contact you as soon as possible. We are all socially committed individuals who understands your feelings and sufferings. We will help you in every possible way we can, to get your life up in the society.
